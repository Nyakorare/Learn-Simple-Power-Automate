Licensing & capacity (overview)

Power Automate is licensed through Microsoft 365, Power Platform, and standalone plans. What you can use depends on your tenant: per-user plans unlock premium connectors for that user; per-flow (where available) targets specific high-scale scenarios.

Run limits and throttling still apply—design flows to avoid unnecessary loops and to batch work where possible.

  • Check your organization’s entitlements before building on premium connectors.
  • Use non-production environments to estimate run consumption while you learn.
